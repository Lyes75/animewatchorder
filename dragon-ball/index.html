Frequently Asked Questions

Should I watch Dragon Ball GT?

GT is optional. It was produced by Toei Animation without direct involvement from Akira Toriyama and is not part of the official canon. Dragon Ball Super effectively replaced GT as the canonical continuation. That said, GT has its moments ‚Äî the Super Saiyan 4 transformation is iconic, and the final episode is genuinely emotional. Watch it if you're a completionist, skip it if you want to stick to canon.

Is Dragon Ball GT canon?

No. Dragon Ball GT is not considered canon. It exists in an alternate timeline separate from the main continuity established by Akira Toriyama. Dragon Ball Super is the official canonical sequel to Dragon Ball Z.

Do I need to watch Dragon Ball before Dragon Ball Z?

Technically no, but strongly recommended. Dragon Ball introduces Goku, the core cast, and the world-building that DBZ builds upon. Many emotional moments in Z hit harder with DB context. If you absolutely can't commit, you can start with Z/Kai, but you'll miss important character development.

Where can I watch all of Dragon Ball?

Crunchyroll has the most complete Dragon Ball library, including Dragon Ball, Dragon Ball Z, Dragon Ball Super, Dragon Ball GT, Dragon Ball Daima, and most of the films. Some films may also be available on Amazon Prime Video or Netflix depending on your region.

How long does it take to watch all of Dragon Ball?

Watching everything (all series + all films) takes approximately 800+ hours. The Essentialist path (canon only) takes about 350 hours. The Standard recommended path takes about 360 hours. The Completionist path with GT and all fillers reaches approximately 900 hours.

What order should I watch the Dragon Ball Super movies?

The four canon DBS-era films should be watched in this order: Battle of Gods (after DBZ/Kai), Resurrection F (after Battle of Gods), Dragon Ball Super: Broly (after DBS episode 131), and Dragon Ball Super: Super Hero (after Broly). Note that Battle of Gods and Resurrection F are retold in DBS episodes 1-27, so you can skip the films if you're watching the series.
